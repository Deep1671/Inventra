## 🎯 What You Can Test

### ✅ After Seeding, You Can:

1. **View Suppliers**
   - See all 10 suppliers in Suppliers page
   - Search by name or email
   - Filter by category
   - View bank details and UPI IDs

2. **Check Products**
   - See all 10 products in Products page
   - Denim Jeans and Standing Desk show RED (low stock)
   - Edit products to see supplier links
   - Check reorder quantities and thresholds

3. **Create Orders**
   - Create purchase order for low stock items
   - Use supplied products and suppliers
   - Track order status (PENDING → ORDERED → DELIVERED)
   - See stock auto-update on delivery

4. **Record Payments**
   - Record payments using bank details provided
   - Use UPI IDs for payment methods
   - See supplier balance auto-update

5. **Test Automation**
   - Products Denim Jeans and Standing Desk will trigger auto-orders
   - Stock below threshold will auto-generate purchase orders
   - System tracks order creation automatically
